2021 Green Season of Hanazono

Booking Now OPEN for Summer 2021 on March 29!

Some ski resorts are still open in the Niseko area, but the snow is melting day by day, new buds are sprouting, and we are excited for the coming of spring! We will finally open bookings for green season activities from March 29th.

Hanazono offers a wide range of outdoor activities for both adults and children!
Explore Hokkaido’s mountains, forests, oceans, and rivers during Niseko’s spectacular green season. Check out what’s happening at Hanazono for summer 2021 below!
